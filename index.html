Who we are

Liberators Youth Empowerment is a non-governmental organisation focused on transforming the lives of vulnerable children in the scholastic system. We address root causes of bullying through empathy, mentorship and restorative practices that treat both those harmed and those who harm with dignity and care.

Our approach combines emotional-intelligence training, conflict-resolution skills and targeted mentorship to build sustainable positive school climates where every learner can thrive.
