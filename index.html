LIBERATORS YOUTH EMPOWERMENT

"Liberated to liberate"

Liberators Youth Empowerment is a non-governmental organization (NGO)
dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children within the scholastic system.
We primarily focus on those who are at risk due to socioeconomic factors and those who are
actively bullied or are displaying bullying behavior in school environments.


Our goal is to create a healthy, hurt-free zone and a sustainable positive
school environment by establishing a respectful, inclusive community of scholars.
We achieve this through comprehensive interventions that address the root causes of conflict.
This involves treating both the bully and the bullied with empathy and understanding,
recognizing the underlying power disparities,
systemic trauma, and the abuse of oppression that often fuel these negative interactions.


By fostering emotional intelligence, teaching conflict resolution skills,
and providing targeted mentorship, our programs aim to break the cycle of
violence and victimization.
This approach will have a profound and measurable positive impact on schools,
resulting in reduced dropout rates, improved academic performance, and a culture
of mutual respect that empowers every student to thrive.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to confront the cycle of bullying and victimization in schools by adopting a two-pronged,
restorative approach-providing empathetic healing for the bullied and targeted intervention for the bully.

We are dedicated to cultivating a school ecosystem defined by mutual respect, emotional intelligence,
and unwavering safety, ensuring every young person has the freedom to learn, thrive intellectually,
and grow into a resilient, empowered citizen
